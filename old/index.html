Skolopendra Studio, based in Olympia, WA, is owned by Emily Kaelin. Emily specializes in surreal, dark, and evocative art using various mediums like painting, sculpture, and installation. The studio's creations are inspired by the beauty and chaos of nature and life, incorporating grotesque and beautiful elements alike.

Emily Kaelin holds a BFA in Painting and Drawing and an MFA from Burren College of Art. Her work has been shown nationally and internationally, with residencies in Iceland and Washington state. Skolopendra Studio also offers commissions for paintings, sculptures, murals, and custom wands.

Emily is also a musician under the project VKVEENA, blending genres like industrial, black metal, and experimental soundscapes. The first VKVEENA EP, "Annihilation," is coming soon.
